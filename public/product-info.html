Product Info

This is a simple pattern to display product information.

The title of the product is "large" and $nc-brand-gray. The description copy is "small" and $nc-brand-gray2.

Standard Tickets

Description of product goes here. Bacon ipsum dolor amet biltong jowl meatball venison rump pig. Short loin ribeye beef ribs brisket pig tail prosciutto short ribs bresaola.

<div class="pnc-roduct-info">
<div class="nc-product-info__title">
Title of product
</div>
<div class="nc-product-info__desc">
<p>Description of product goes here.</p>
</div>
</div>

Compact Product Information

There can be scenarios where the large size of product tile doesn't work. In those cases, the product title is the same size as the description (small).

Standard Tickets

Description of product goes here. Bacon ipsum dolor amet biltong jowl meatball venison rump pig. Short loin ribeye beef ribs brisket pig tail prosciutto short ribs bresaola.

<div class="product-info">
<div class="nc-product-info__compact-title">
Title of product
</div>
<div class="nc-product-info__desc">
<p>Description of product goes here.</p>
</div>
</div>

Note that we are not using a BEM modifier class here.
